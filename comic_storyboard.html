Learning to Read – Story Levels

Start at Level 1 with very easy words. Turn the page to read harder words.

Read the story from Level 1 to Level 5. Each level has the same idea with harder words.

Level 1 Very easy words

Short sentences Simple sight words
Story

I am Sam. I see a dog. The dog is big. I see a red ball. I can get the ball.

Words in this level:
I, am, Sam, see, a, dog, the, is, big, red, ball, can, get

Level 2 Easy sentences

More detail A few new words
Story

I am Sam. I see a big brown dog in the yard. The dog has a red ball. I run to the dog. The dog drops the ball. I pick up the ball and smile.

New words in this level:
brown, yard, has, run, drops, pick, smile

Level 3 Medium sentences

Full sentences Connectors like “and”, “but”
Story

My name is Sam and I like to play outside. A big brown dog runs to me with a red ball in his mouth. I want to throw the ball, but the grass is wet. I walk slowly so I do not slip, and the dog waits for me. I throw the ball far, and the happy dog races after it.

New words in this level:
name, like, outside, mouth, want, throw, grass, wet, slowly, slip, waits, far, races, after, happy

Level 4 Longer sentences

Short paragraph Describing feelings
Story

Every afternoon, I meet the big brown dog at the yard gate. He carries his red ball and wags his tail because he knows we will play. I feel excited when I throw the ball high into the bright sky and watch it fall. The dog jumps, catches the ball, and trots back to me, proud and panting. We rest in the soft grass for a moment before we start another game.

New words in this level:
every, afternoon, gate, carries, wags, because, excited, high, bright, sky, watch, fall, jumps, catches, trots, proud, panting, rest, soft, moment, another

Level 5 Short story paragraph

More complex ideas Richer vocabulary
Story

After school, I hurry home because I know the big brown dog is waiting for me with his red ball. The day is cool and breezy, so the leaves dance while we play in the yard. I throw the ball again and again until my arms feel tired, but the dog still runs with endless energy. When the sun begins to set and the sky turns orange, we finally slow down and sit together in the quiet grass. I pet his warm fur and feel calm, knowing we will play this same happy game again tomorrow.

New words in this level:
after, school, hurry, waiting, cool, breezy, leaves, dance, again, until, tired, endless, energy, begins, set, orange, finally, slow, quiet, calm, tomorrow
